Data cleaning and preprocessing are vital steps to ensure the accuracy and reliability of GIS analysis. This section will cover methods for handling missing data, transforming and normalizing datasets, and preparing data for effective analysis. Proper preprocessing improves data quality and enhances the results of GIS projects.

- **Handling Missing Data**
  - Techniques for identifying and dealing with missing or incomplete data.
- **Data Transformation**
  - Methods for transforming data to fit the required format and structure.
- **Data Normalization**
  - Techniques for normalizing data to ensure consistency and comparability.
